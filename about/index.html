Meet Zor

Seattle perspective, modern execution

As a fourth-generation Seattleite, I’ve watched our neighborhoods evolve and know where the next opportunities are surfacing. Before real estate I spent years at Amazon and built, scaled, and sold a few businesses, which taught me how to think strategically, move quickly, and negotiate with clarity. That same mindset now guides buyers, sellers, and investors through every step.

I pair hyper-local knowledge with a designer’s eye, sharp marketing, and a workflow that keeps your priorities front and center. You’ll always know what’s next, why it matters, and what it takes to win.

Off the clock, you’ll usually find me on a bike, exploring the Northwest, or hanging out with my wife, daughter, and our two dogs.

Seller Guide

Sell your home in Seattle

Plan, prep, market, and negotiate with clarity so your sale stays organized and confident from start to finish.

1

Strategy consult & value range

Review comps, condition, and timing to set a smart price band and launch plan.

30–45 min

We align on what the market will reward and where to avoid over-spend.

  • Comparable sales, active competition, and absorption (pace of sales).
  • Target buyer profile and seasonal timing windows.
  • Net sheet overview: typical seller costs and estimated proceeds.
2

Prep plan: repairs, paint, staging

Tidy list with ROI in mind — light updates and staging usually outperform heavy remodels.

1–3 weeks
  • High-ROI touches: paint, lighting, landscaping, deep clean, minor fixes.
  • Optional pre-inspection to reduce unknowns and speed negotiations.
  • Staging scope matched to home style and price point.
3

Media & marketing launch

Pro photos, floor plan, video, and targeted distribution to drive day-one traffic.

3–5 days
  • Photography, floor plan, measured square footage verification when relevant.
  • Listing copy that highlights value drivers and reduces buyer uncertainty.
  • MLS syndication, portals, social, email to buyer agents, and coming-soon (where allowed).
4

On-market: showings & feedback

Make access easy, track interest, and react to the market in real time.

days 1–14
  • Showing strategy (grouped windows vs. flexible access) to concentrate activity.
  • Agent feedback loop: adjust small blockers fast (lighting, access notes, disclosures).
  • Weekly market report: traffic, saves, comp changes, and price-cut watch.
5

Offer review & negotiation

Compare price and terms — certainty and speed can beat headline number.

same day
  • Matrix review: price, earnest money, financing, closing date, contingencies.
  • Counter strategies: response order, escalation mechanics, appraisal gap language.
  • Pick the offer that maximizes net with lowest fall-through risk.
6

Under contract: appraisal to closing

Manage timelines, docs, and repairs so we close cleanly and on schedule.

3–5 weeks
  • Disclosures, title/escrow, appraisal coordination, repair negotiations if needed.
  • Clear to close checklist: payoff, utilities, move-out plan, key transfer.
  • Final walk-through support and settlement statement review.
